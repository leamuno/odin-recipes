Tamago Kake Gohan

Description

A wonderfully simple yet delicious Japanese dish. This recipe will create two servings.

Ingredients

  • 2 - High quality eggs
  • 1 - Cup of short grain rice
  • 1 - Cup of water
  • 1 - Stalk of green onion
  • 2 - Table spoons of dark soy sauce
  • 2 - Table spoons of furikake (optional)

Directions

  1. Wash rice until water runs clear
  2. Add rice to the cooking method of choice alongside 1 cup of water. A rice cooker is recommended.
  3. Slice the green onion as thinly as possible
  4. After the rice is prepared, split into two bowls
  5. Crack the egg over the rice and add the soy sauce to each bowl
  6. Mix vigorously.
  7. Sprinkle the sliced green onion over top as well as the furikake if desired.
  8. Enjoy!
