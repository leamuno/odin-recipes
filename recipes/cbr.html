Chicken, Broccoli, and Rice

Description

A wonderfully simple yet delicious dish for the gym bro. This recipe will create two servings.

Ingredients

  • 4 - Chicken tenderloins
  • 1 - Cup of long grain rice
  • 1 - Cup of water
  • 1 - Teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1 - Teaspoon of cumin
  • 1 - Teaspoon of paprika
  • 1 - Teaspoon of chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Spray oil of choice
  • Frozen bag of broccoli

Directions

  1. Wash rice until water runs clear
  2. Add rice to the cooking method of choice alongside 1 cup of water. A rice cooker is recommended.
  3. Add chicken to a bowl along with all spices.
  4. Mix thorougly then spray a light coating of oil on each piece.
  5. Place chicken in the air fryer or preheated oven.
  6. Flip the chicken halfway through
  7. Toss the bag of frozen broccoli into a microwave and follow the package instructions.
  8. Once everything is prepared, spilt everything evenly into 2 containers of your choice.
  9. Add salt and pepper to taste
  10. Enjoy!
