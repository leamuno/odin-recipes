Egg Fried Rice

Description

A wonderfully simple yet delicious Chinese dish. This recipe will create two servings.

Ingredients

  • 2 - Eggs
  • 1 - Cup of long grain rice
  • 1 - Cup of water
  • 2 - Cloves of Garlic
  • 2 - Stalk of green onion
  • 2 - Table spoons of dark soy sauce
  • 2 - Table spoons of vegetable oil
  • 1 - Teaspoon of MSG
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Wash rice until water runs clear
  2. Add rice to the cooking method of choice alongside 1 cup of water. A rice cooker is recommended.
  3. Refrigerate rice overnight. Fresh cooked rice is not recommended in this recipe
  4. Slice the green onion as thinly as possible separate the whites from the greens
  5. Mince the garlic very finely
  6. Heat up a steel pan or wok and wait for it to become ripping hot.
  7. Season the pan with oil to make it non-stick.
  8. Drop in the whites of the spring onion followed by the garlic until fragrant.
  9. Push the aromatics to the side of the pan and add the eggs.
  10. After the eggs are thoroughly scrambled add the rice and mix well.
  11. Turn off the heat then add the msg and soy sauce.
  12. Lastly toss in the sesame and the green part of the green onioin.
  13. Add salt and pepper to taste
  14. Enjoy!
